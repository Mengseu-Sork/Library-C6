Study Space in the Library

1. Capacity: 15 people

2. Purpose: A quiet area designated for focused study, research, or reading.

Library Rules

1. No Food or Drink: Patrons are not allowed to bring food or beverages into the library to maintain cleanliness and avoid damage to materials.

2. Silence Policy: Conversations are not permitted to ensure a quiet atmosphere for everyone. This encourages concentration and respect for others' study needs.

3. No Theft: All library materials, including books and media, must be respected and returned. Stealing or damaging library property is strictly prohibited.
